How to Get Your House Ready to Sell

First impressions matter. Follow our proven checklist to prepare your home for maximum appeal and faster sales.

1

Deep Clean & Declutter

Remove personal items, excess furniture, and deep clean every room. A clean, spacious home appears larger and more appealing to buyers.

2

Minor Repairs & Updates

Fix leaky faucets, squeaky doors, and broken fixtures. Consider fresh paint, updated hardware, and modern lighting fixtures.

3

Curb Appeal Enhancement

Landscape the front yard, clean the exterior, paint the front door, and ensure the entrance is welcoming and well-lit.

4

Professional Staging

Arrange furniture to maximize space, add neutral decor, and create inviting living areas that help buyers envision themselves in the home.

5

Professional Photography

High-quality photos showcase your home's best features and attract more potential buyers online.

6

Pre-Listing Inspection

Address any major issues before listing to avoid surprises during the buyer's inspection and ensure a smooth transaction.
